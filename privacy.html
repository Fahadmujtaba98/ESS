1. Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing

Purpose of Processing Lawful Basis

2. Categories of Personal Data Collected

  • Identification Data: Employee ID, full name, designation, department
  • Contact Information: Official email, mobile number
  • Employment & HR Records: Attendance logs, leave balances, salary slips (view-only), shift schedules
  • Technical & Security Data: Device details, login timestamps, IP address

3. Data Storage and Security Measures

  • Encrypted communication (HTTPS/SSL) for all data transfers
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) to restrict access to authorized personnel only
  • Audit logs and monitoring to track access or misuse attempts
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Disclosure

Recipient Purpose of Access
Internal HR & Payroll Teams Processing attendance, leave, payroll information
Internal IT/Security Teams System monitoring, fraud prevention
Government or Legal Authorities Only when legally required

5. Data Retention Policy

  • Active employee data will be retained for the duration of employment.
  • Upon resignation or termination, your ESS account will be deactivated.
  • Data will be archived or deleted as per legal retention policies.

6. Data Subject Rights

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of stored data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or outdated data
  • Right to Restriction: Limit data usage in certain cases
  • Right to Complaint: Report misuse or unauthorized access
