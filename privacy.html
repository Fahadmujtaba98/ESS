2. Categories of Personal Data Collected

  • Identification Data: Employee ID, full name, designation, department, branch/store information.
  • Contact Information: Official email address and mobile number.
  • Approval & Workflow Data: Approval requests, approval history, comments, workflow status, timestamps, and related business records.
  • Technical & Security Data: Device information, browser information, IP address, login records, and application usage logs.

3. Data Storage and Security Measures

  • Encrypted communication (HTTPS/SSL) for all data transfers.
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) to ensure authorized access only.
  • Comprehensive audit logs for approval actions and system activity.
  • Regular security monitoring and vulnerability assessments.
  • Secure hosting infrastructure with backup and disaster recovery procedures.

5. Data Retention Policy

  • Approval records will be retained according to company retention policies.
  • User accounts will remain active during employment.
  • Upon resignation or termination, access to Smart Approval will be revoked.
  • Archived records may be retained for legal, audit, and compliance purposes.

6. User Rights

  • Right to Access: Request information regarding personal data processed within the system.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Restriction: Request limited processing where legally applicable.
  • Right to Report Concerns: Report privacy concerns, misuse, or unauthorized access.
