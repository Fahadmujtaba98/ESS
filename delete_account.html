Account Deletion Policy • Last Updated: [Insert Date]

1. Requesting Account Deletion

If you wish to permanently delete your KFC Pakistan – ESS mobile application account, please send an official email request from your registered company email address.

Send your account deletion request to:

Email: hrsupport@kfcpakistan.com

Subject: Request to Delete ESS Account

Include the following details in your email:

  • Full Name
  • Employee ID
  • Registered Email Address
  • Department

2. Account Deletion Process

  • Your request will be verified by the HR & IT team for authenticity.
  • Once verified, your ESS account will be marked for deletion.
  • The deletion process will be completed within 40 working days from the date of request.

3. Data Removal Policy

  • All associated personal data, including attendance, leave, and shift details, will be permanently removed from the application database after account deletion.
  • Archived HR records required by law (e.g., payroll, tax compliance) may be retained for legal or auditing purposes.
  • Once the deletion process is complete, reactivation of the same account will not be possible.

4. Confirmation

Upon successful deletion, you will receive an official confirmation email from hrsupport@kfcpakistan.com.
